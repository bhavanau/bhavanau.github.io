imperfect: a collection of prose and poetry

imperfect defines the journey of a wilting rose, petals discarded and color faded, that beings to thrive again with the lessons she learned in her 23 years of existence.

you are a rose
do not shed your petals
for those who will not bleed
- bhavana

about the author

bhavana uppalapati is a chemistry researcher by day, an avid author by night. hailing from ontario, canada, covid-19 resulted in a quarantine that forced bhavana to pick up her pen and sketch the words she bottled in for twenty-three years. as a strong believer that words can save lives, she birthed he debut collection of prose and poetry , imperfect in 2021. get in touch at bhavana_u@hotmail.com.
