about the author

bhavana uppalapati is a chemist by day, an avid author by night.

hailing from ontario, canada, covid-19 resulted in a quarantine that forced bhavana to pick up her pen and sketch the words she bottled in for twenty-three years. as a strong believer that words can save lives, she birthed her debut collection of prose and poetry, imperfect, in 2021.
